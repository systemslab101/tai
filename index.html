Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need ChatGPT or Claude? Does it matter?
We believe everyone should have a paid plan for the AI tool they use most. We’ve tested this over and over, and the jump in quality is significant once you move from free to paid. The specific tool matters less than having a paid version you’ll consistently use well.
Do I need to pay for other tools?
Not necessarily. You can get a lot of value with just your paid AI tool. If you start building automations, you may eventually choose tools like Make or Zapier, but that part is optional.
Is this for TPT creators only?
No. This is for educators building any kind of education business. You do not need to have a TPT store.
Why join a community instead of learning alone?
Because we tried that. There’s a difference between watching and implementing. Inside this community, we’re building real systems inside real businesses and opening that process up to others.
What exactly do I get?
You’ll work through real business challenges, get founder feedback, join a monthly live call, and see the biggest community breakthroughs each month.
What level is this for?
Best fit: educators who already have a paid AI plan and want to go beyond casual prompting into workflows, systems, and better business outcomes.
Will you give us prompts?
Sometimes. But this community is about systems, workflows, implementation, and outcomes, not collecting prompts for the sake of it.
Are there courses to follow?
We plan to build focused mini-courses around the most useful and most-requested community breakthroughs.
Who is behind this community?
Mike focuses on tech and security. Katie focuses on AI and product systems. Rachel focuses on community and growth. Together, we each bring different strengths from real education businesses already using AI every week.
